Property dimension

Common vs Private

The property dimension is a measure of how land is distributed in society. It ranges from common property, where natural resources are shared by all, to private property, where nature is appropriated by private legal persons.


Production dimension

Socialized vs Personalized

The production dimension is a measure of how production is organized in society. It includes:
Socialized production, where the products of labor are managed by society,
Personalized production, where wealth advanced for the production of more wealth meet the personal wants of legal persons.
